Walter White - Heisenberg

Walter White – A high school chemistry teacher turned ruthless drug kingpin. After being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, he begins manufacturing meth to secure his family’s future but gradually becomes consumed by power and ego. Operating under the alias "Heisenberg," he transforms from a desperate man into a calculating and dangerous criminal, willing to do anything to maintain control.

x

Jesse Pinkman – Cap’n Cook

Jesse Pinkman – A former student of Walter White and his partner in the meth business. Jesse is a troubled but kind-hearted individual who struggles with guilt, addiction, and moral dilemmas throughout the series. Despite his flaws, he has a strong sense of loyalty and a desire for redemption.

x

Saul Goodman – Slippin’ Jimmy

Saul Goodman – A shady but charismatic criminal lawyer who helps Walter and Jesse navigate the drug trade. Beneath his flashy persona, Saul (real name Jimmy McGill) is a clever and resourceful lawyer who bends the law to his advantage, often prioritizing self-preservation over morality.

x

Mike Ehrmantraut – The Fixer

Mike Ehrmantraut – A former cop turned enforcer for Gus Fring. Mike is highly skilled, disciplined, and pragmatic, handling security, assassinations, and logistics for the drug empire. Despite his criminal ties, he follows a strict personal code and cares deeply for his granddaughter.

x

Hank Schrader – ASAC Schrader

Hank Schrader – A DEA agent and Walter White’s brother-in-law. Hank is bold, determined, and relentless in his pursuit of justice, often acting as the moral opposite of Walt. His investigation into Heisenberg’s identity becomes one of the key tensions in the series, leading to intense personal and professional consequences.
